Our Core Values

Public Safety

Our Value: Everyone deserves to live in a safe community free from fear.

Our Stance: Public safety should protect lives while respecting responsible gun ownership.

Current Issue: The U.S. faces a gun violence crisis, with rising deaths since the pandemic.

Policies We Will Pass: Universal background checks and a ban on assault-style weapons.

Housing & Security

Our Value: Safe, affordable housing is a basic human need.

Our Stance: Housing stability creates better opportunities and stronger communities.

Current Issue: Homelessness and lack of affordable housing continue to rise nationwide.

Policies We Will Pass: Expand housing funding, rental assistance, and support services.

Justice & Peaceful Policy

Our Value: Safety and justice must respect human rights and international law.

Our Stance: Cooperation and diplomacy create lasting security.

Current Issue: U.S. military actions abroad raise concerns over legality and stability.

Policies We Will Pass: International cooperation and shared anti-trafficking strategies.
